"Obsolete" Document Status

draft

RB , 1995-08-14

.meaning : The "obsolete" document status indicates that the contents are not current and are not necessarily consistent with the rest of the system, sources, or products.

.use : When a document goes out of date, and is it is no longer useful to maintain it, then it should be marked as obsolete.

.rule.source : Obsolete documents must not be used as sources of information.

.rule.quote : The tag of an obsolete document may be quoted elsewhere, but where it is clear that it is being referred to as such.

.rule.tag : The tag of an obsolete document should not be changed without informing everyone who might know about the document and searching for cross references in other documents.
