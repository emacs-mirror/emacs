Person

draft

RB , 1995-08-14

Purpose

The person document type records information about members of the Memory Management staff. The existence of a person document allows the person to be recorded in documents.

Content

The person form includes a field for the person's full name, and a space for notes.

Tagging

Documents of type person must be tagged "person.<code>" where <code> is the userid of the person.
