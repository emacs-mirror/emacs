Purpose

A bibliography entry records information about a publication, including reviews and notes about the publication's relevance to the project.

Content

The form includes standard publication details, and also space for a copy of the abstract, and for reviews and comments.

Tagging

Tags for bibiography entries are derived from the type of publication, the authors names, and the publication date. For example, a paper by Paul Wilson published in 1995 is tagged "paper.wil95". A book by Tom Gilb published in 1989 is tagged "book.gilb89".
