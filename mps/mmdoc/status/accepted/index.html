"Accepted" Document Status

draft

RB , 1995-08-14

.meaning : An "accepted" document has been completed by the author, and has passed review and been accepted (see proc.review). Since the document has passed the exit criteria for review of a document of its type, it has a fairly accurate prediction of the number of defects remaining. Its contents may be considered reliable.

.use : Mark a document as accepted _only_ when it has passed through the review process successfully.

.rule.source : An accepted document can and should be used as a source of information.

.rule.quote : The tag of an accepted can be quoted, because the tag shouldn't change.

.rule.tag : The tag of an accepted document should not be changed without informing everyone who might know about the document and searching for cross references in other documents.
