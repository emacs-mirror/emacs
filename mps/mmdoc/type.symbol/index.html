Interface Symbol

draft

GRM , 1997-04-15

Purpose

To document symbols exported to the client of a product.

Content

Details of a function, constant, etc., formally, in English, and with reference to related symbols.

.notes: The Internal Notes field is for notes that will not be published to external clients.

Tagging

Symbols are tagged symbol.<product>.<language>.<symbol>, where <product> is the product exporting the symbol, <language> is the language whose binding is being described, and <symbol> is the symbol. Note that <symbol> should have the same spelling as in the program, if at all possible.
