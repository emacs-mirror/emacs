Pre-processor symbol: MPS_BUILD_DC


This refers to the DIGITAL C compiler shipped with DIGITAL UNIX (aka OSF/1 
V4.0).

The options we use are gleaned from the cc man page on schiele (uname -a: OSF1 
schiele.cam.harlequin.co.uk V4.0 386 alpha)

CC = cc
CFLAGSCOMPILER = -std1 -w2 -warnprotos -portable -readonly_strings
CFLAGSDEBUG = -g2
CFLAGSOPT = -O2 -g3
CFLAGSOPTNODEBUG = -O2
