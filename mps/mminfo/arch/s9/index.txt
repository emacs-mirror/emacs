UltraSPARCs are a family of processor produced by Sun, implementing Sparc v9, 
compatible with SPARC v8 (arch.s8). Sun make compilers (SunPro, see builder.sc) 
which specialize on UltraSPARCs; in order to build ScriptWorks using these 
compilers the MPS has to detect this target independently.
