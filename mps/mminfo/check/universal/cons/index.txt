                            incomplete check

Scope: This checklist is for use in reviews of all documents, using the consistency 
role (see rule.universal.cons).

Summary: Is the document consistent with itself, other (accepted) documents, and its purpose?


.self: Where similar things are said in two places in the product document, do 
they use  a common definition where appropriate, or are they as similar as 
possible otherwise?

.other: Where portions of the product document correspond to portions of source 
documents, do they use a common definition where appropriate, or do they 
correspond as closely as possible otherwise.

.purp: Is all of the product document entirely consistent with its (stated) 
purpose?
