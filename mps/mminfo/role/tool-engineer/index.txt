                     TOOL DEVELOPER AND MAINTAINER
                           role.tool-engineer
                               draft role
                             drj 1995-10-23


Person :

drj


Description:

Responsible for the tools that the group uses.  Ensuring that the group's 
members have adequate tools, and that the tools are installed properly, and 
that the tools are adequately documented for the group (in general the 
documentation provided with tools will be too general).  This will also involve 
evaluation, construction, and acquisition of tools.
