                    PROCEDURE FOR INSPECTION LEADER
                        book.gilb93.proc.leader
                              draft quote
                          Tom Gilb 1993-01-01

1. Plan the process:
    a. assemble a team;
    b. assign roles to team members;
    c. identify relevant documents;
    d. determine optimum rates;
    e. determine meeting places and times.
2. Lead meetings.
3. Collect basic metrics.
4. Make sure rules and procedures are followed.
