                    PROCEDURE FOR LEADER AT KICKOFF
                    book.gilb93.proc.leader.kickoff
                              draft quote
                          Tom Gilb 1993-01-01

1. Distribute documents.
2. Ask if any questions as to master plan.
3. Train novices on rules, procedures, checklists.
4. Get team to agree on kickoff objective (numeric).
5. Get team to agree on kickoff strategy.
