                       THE "GUIDE" DOCUMENT TYPE
                               type.guide
                               draft type
                           richard 1995-08-27

Rules: rule.universal

Tag template: guide.<procedure>(.<name>)*


Purpose:

A guide is a document which contains guidelines for carrying out a procedure.


Content:

Guidelines do not tell you how to do something step by step (for that see 
proc.*), but contain discursive and descriptive information about a particular 
part of development.
