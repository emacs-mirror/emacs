                                 PERSON
                              type.person
                               draft type
                           richard 1995-08-14

Rules: rule.universal

Tag template: person.<code>


Tagging:

Documents of type person must be tagged "person.<code>" where <code> is the 
userid of the person.


Purpose:

The person document type records information about members of the Memory 
Management staff.  The existence of a person document allows the person to be 
recorded in documents.


Content:

The person form includes a field for the person's full name, and a space for 
notes.


Procedures:

proc.person
