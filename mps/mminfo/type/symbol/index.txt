                            INTERFACE SYMBOL
                              type.symbol
                               draft type
                           gavinm 1997-04-15

Rules: rule.universal

Tag template: symbol.<product>.<language>.<symbol>


Tagging:

Symbols are tagged symbol.<product>.<language>.<symbol>, where <product> is the 
product exporting the symbol, <language> is the language whose binding is being 
described, and <symbol> is the symbol.  Note that <symbol> should have the same 
spelling as in the program, if at all possible.


Purpose:

To document symbols exported to the client of a product.


Content:

Details of a function, constant, etc., formally, in English, and with reference 
to related symbols.
