               REQUIREMENTS SATISFACTION CHART FOR DYLAN
                            plan.dylan.chart
                             temporary doc
                             drj 1996-11-06

.readership: anyone interested in the progress of the MM/Dylan Project, and all 
MM group members.

.purpose: The purpose of this document is to indicate what (Dylan) requirements 
are met at what times and what requirements are intended to be met.

.source: plan.dylan, req.dylan

.def.d*: d* is a sequence of dates at which times the status of the project is 
recorded.

.def.d0: d0 is the date 1996-11-04

.def.buffalo: Buffalo is the name of a release.  See version.dylan.buffalo, 
plan.dylan, project.dylan, etc.

.key:

for the d* columns (there is only one at the moment)

/ requirement satisfied
- requirement partially satisfied
? requirement satisfaction unknown
> attribute requirement not satisfied
  requirement not satisfied
* alert

for the Buffalo column

/ intend to satisfy requirement
  do not intend to satisfy requirement
. intend that the extent to which an attribute requirement is met is known
? do not intend that implied by '.'
* do not intent to satisfy requirement and this is required for next release
