                          RULES FOR CHECKLISTS
                               rule.check
                               draft rule
                           gavinm 1996-06-26

Scope: These rules are to be used when writing review checklists.


.rule: Each checklist question must be derived from a referenced rule.

.question: Each checklist question must be a question.

.negative: A negative answer to the question should indicate an issue.

.major: Checklist questions should be directed primarily at major issues (see 
guide.review.class.major).

.common: Checklist questions should be directed primarily at common issues 
(subject to .major).

.brief: Checklists must be no more than one page long; no more than 12 
questions.
