                      RULES OF WRITING GLOSSARIES
                             rule.glossary
                               draft rule
                           gavinm 1996-11-25

Scope: These rules apply to any glossary.


.comp: The entries should be readily comprehensible to the intended readership.

.acc: The entries should be accurate.

.useful: The entries should be useful to the intended readership, when working 
in the intended field.
