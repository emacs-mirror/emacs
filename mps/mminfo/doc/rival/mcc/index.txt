MCC is an experimental mostly-copying collector built by Frederick Smith and 
Greg Morrisett in 1999 to test the ideas of mostly-copying.  It only needs one 
pass, unlike Bartlett, but there are no numbers yet on the performance.

It is available at <URL:http://www.cs.cornell.edu/Info/People/fms/MCC/gc.html>.
