                      ABSTRACT COLLECTION PROCESS
                              idea.collect
                               draft idea
                           richard 1995-07-28

SUMMARY

A "collection is the temporal instantiation of a "collector".  In
other words, it is the process of performing a cycle of garbage
collection.


DESCRIPTION

1. "Flip"
   a. condemn a sub-graph
   b. set colour to white
   c. scan the atomic roots

2. "Trace"
   a. choose something to scan from the greylist
   b. scan it, turning it black
   c. fix objects, turning them grey

3. "Reclaim"
   a. recycle remaining white objects
