     5  *
     6  * .rationale: The generic parts of the MPS do not call the ANSI C
     7  * library directly, because it is required to run in a freestanding
     8  * environment (standard.ansic section 5.1.2.1).  The lib module
     9  * provides an ANSI library-like interface which can be replaced.
    10  *
    11  * .id: The identifiers in this interface are, as far as possible,
    12  * the same as those in the ANSI standard, except that identifiers
    13  * are prefixed with "Lib_".
    14  *
    15  * .ref: Section numbers referred to in this header are references
    16  * to the ANSI C standard (standard.ansic) unless otherwise stated.
    17  *
    18  * .headers: This header includes the headers that should be
    19  * available in the freestanding environment (standard.ansic section
    20  * 4) except float.h, because floats are inherently evil.
    21  */
    22

    24 #define lib_h
