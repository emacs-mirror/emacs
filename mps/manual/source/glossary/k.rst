.. _glossary-k:

===========
Glossary: K
===========

.. glossary::

    kB

        .. see:: :term:`kilobyte`.

    kilobyte

        .. aka:: *kB*.

        A kilobyte is 1024 :term:`bytes (1) <byte (1)>`.

        See :term:`byte (1)` for general information on this and
        related quantities.

        The standard abbreviation is "kB", but "KB" is often used by
        people unfamiliar with the metric system.
